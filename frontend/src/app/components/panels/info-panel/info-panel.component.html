Barents watch

This app was created to track Norwegian vessels. On the map there are circles in different colors which represent numbers of vessels in this region:

  • purple - 1-5 vessels
  • red - 6-10 vessels
  • yellow - 11-20 vessels
  • blue - above 20 vessels

At closed range vessels are treated separately (not in groups). This allows to check if the actual vessel position was registered earlier than 5 minutes ago (it is marked as grey circle, otherwise is green circle).

Logged user can add single vessels to track them individually to have history of their positions while they was being tracked. There is also possibility to suspend tracking, resume it or even remove all history about vessel positions.

On the map tracked vessels are marked as blue polylines with circles on current positions. If tracking is suspended then color of polyline is grey. Suspended tracking means that application will not record positions of this vessel. If tracking is resumed then polyline since suspension to renewal will be red and dashed.
